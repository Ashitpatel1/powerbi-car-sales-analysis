# powerbi-car-sales-analysis

Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution built using Power BI to analyze car sales performance across time, regions, dealers, and product categories.
The dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable insights using interactive visualizations, KPIs, and DAX measures, enabling data-driven decision-making for business stakeholders.
The report consists of:
Overview Dashboard ‚Äì Executive-level KPIs and trends
Details Dashboard ‚Äì Transaction-level drill-down analysis

Business Objectives

Monitor Year-to-Date (YTD) and Month-to-Date (MTD) sales performance
Analyze pricing trends and sales volume
Identify top-performing regions, brands, and body styles
Enable drill-down analysis for operational decision-making
Support strategic planning using data-driven insights

Key KPIs & Metrics

YTD Total Sales
MTD Total Sales
YTD Average Selling Price
MTD Average Selling Price
YTD Cars Sold
MTD Cars Sold

Period-over-Period Growth (%)

 Key Insights

Strong Revenue Performance
YTD total sales exceeded $370M, indicating consistent business growth.
High-Revenue Product Categories
SUVs and Sedans contribute the majority of total sales revenue.
Customer Color Preferences Matter
Neutral colors (White, Black) generate higher sales volumes.
Regional Sales Disparities
Certain dealer regions consistently outperform others.
Brand Contribution Concentration
A small set of companies contributes a significant share of overall revenue.
Stable Pricing Trends
Average selling price remains stable with minimal variation.

 Business Recommendations

Prioritize inventory and marketing for high-performing body styles.
Strengthen dealer operations in top-performing regions.
Review pricing strategies for underperforming brands.
Use drill-down analysis to optimize dealer-level performance.
Monitor pricing trends to maintain profitability and competitiveness.


 Conclusion

This project demonstrates strong proficiency in Power BI, DAX, data visualization, and business analytics.
It showcases the ability to convert raw sales data into clear, actionable business insights, making it a portfolio-ready project suitable for Data Analyst / Business Analyst roles
